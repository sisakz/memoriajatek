HUNGARY WELCOMES EUROSKILLS IN BUDAPEST

At 4th December 2016, at the Closing Ceremony of EuroSkills Göteborg 2016 the organizers handed over the flag of EuroSkills to the next host – Hungary. The organizers of EuroSkills Budapest 2018 proudly welcome the competition in Budapest for the first time in Central Eastern Europe between 26 and 28 September 2018.

The EuroSkills competition is an outstanding opportunity to draw the attention of pupils and their parents to the vocational training opportunities, and will provide an excellent possibility to improve the reputation and recognition of the Euroskills movement in Hungary, and successfully contributes giving due respect to skilled work.

Let’s get down to work, time to take on the challenge! Budapest is looking forward to welcoming the competitors, experts and everybody interested in the competition from all around Europe.

While counting back we are inviting you to play. The game introduces different skilles of the upcoming EuroSkills competition. Have a fun!
